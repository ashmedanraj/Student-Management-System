Welcome to the Chapman University Management System

The Chapman University Management System is a web-based platform designed to manage college records and academic information efficiently. This dashboard serves as the central control panel where users can access different sections such as student details, course information, and contact support. The system allows administrators to add, edit, and manage student data easily while keeping the information organized and accessible. With a clean and simple interface, this platform helps improve the management of student records in an effective and structured way.
